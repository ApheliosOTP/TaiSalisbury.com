02 — Values

Mission &
Values Statement

I was drawn towards Exercise Science and Psychology because I know that peak human performance is not achievable from merely physical improvements. It is a product of the mind, the body, and the choices one makes when no one else is watching.

“I will become someone who follows that conviction. I hold myself to this high standard because others deserve someone who does.”

Throughout my career, I intend to bring advancements that give individuals the tools to thrive in body and mind. My goals are to acquire the knowledge required to fulfill my passions, contribute to the researching and coaching field, and develop systems or approaches that help people unlock their full potential.

FieldPsychology
